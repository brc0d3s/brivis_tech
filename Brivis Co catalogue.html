Q: What is Electronic Products?

A: For over 50 years known as the design engineer’s magazine of product technology, Electronic Products specializes in reporting on new components used in the design of electronic equipment of all types. We cover products in the following categories:

  • Analog / Mixed Signal ICs
  • Board Level Products
  • Computer Peripherals
  • Digital ICs
  • Discrete Semiconductors
  • Electromechanical Components
  • Electronics Packaging
  • Interconnections
  • Components
  • Passive Components
  • Power Products
  • RF / Microwave Components
  • Sensors and Transducers
  • Software
  • Test and Measurement

We provide articles on the latest product technology, specific product topics, noteworthy new products, and the latest new products in categorized New Products sections. Each edition also includes a Meetings section to help the engineer plan a schedule of meetings and conferences to attend.

Q: How do I buy a part from you?

A: You don’t. Electronic Products is a magazine that specializes in reporting on new products for the electronics design engineer. We don’t sell products ourselves. We leave that to the companies we include in our magazine and in our product database on the Web, where we provide company contact information by telephone, fax, e-mail, and Web site.
